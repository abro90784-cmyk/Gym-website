Our Story

BUILT FOR CHAMPIONS

Founded in 2009 in Karachi, IronForge started as a small boxing gym with a big vision. Today we've grown to 5 locations across Pakistan, serving over 2,500 members daily with world-class facilities and expert coaching.

Our philosophy is simple: Every person deserves access to professional-grade fitness training. We believe transformation happens not just in the body, but in the mind and spirit.
